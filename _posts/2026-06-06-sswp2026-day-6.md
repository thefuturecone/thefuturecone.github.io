After five hectic days of talks, discussions, and lab activities at the [ICTS-SSWP 2026](https://www.icts.res.in/program/SWWP2026)[^1], it was a much-needed break for everyone. We used the day in different ways - some explored various parts of Bengaluru, including a visit to the ICTS campus.

Apart from relaxing and running errands, the day also gave us a chance to reflect on the sessions and experiences of the past week. We had interesting conversations and new perspectives on the topics we had been learning.

I also spent time on exploring the Collatz conjecture from new perspectives. Will share my observations soon. (Updated: You can see some of my observations on Collatz Conjecture [here](https://thefuturecone.com/2026/06/26/insights-on-collatz.html).)

It was also an opportunity to recharge and get ready for the busy and exciting week ahead.
