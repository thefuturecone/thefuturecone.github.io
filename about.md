## Why "The Future Cone"?

The future cone (the future light cone) is part of the Minkowski Spacetime concept (developed by Hermann Minkowski) which treats space-time as a single four dimensional fabric. This visual map represents all the possible moments, events, and places in the universe that one can possibly reach or influence looking from the current position. The speed of light determines the boundary, implying that all the events outside the boundary can neither influence us nor can we influence them.

I named my site *The Future Cone* as I am interested in exploring the underlying logic and laws that determine possible future states. At the same time, I am interested in understanding the very existence of the boundaries that limit the possibilities. This website captures my thoughts, notes, and ideas on these topics.
